About Unified SME Grants & Loans

Unified SME is dedicated to fostering the growth and development of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) across Nigeria. As a direct affiliate of the Federal Government, we provide a streamlined platform for eligible businesses to access vital financial assistance through various grants and loans. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs, stimulate economic growth, and create sustainable job opportunities without the burden of collaterals and with fast disbursement.

Government Loan Activities

The Federal Government of Nigeria is committed to supporting its vibrant SME sector. Through initiatives like Unified SME, significant capital is disbursed to stimulate innovation, expansion, and job creation. These activities include:

  • Providing direct financial aid to strategic industries.
  • Facilitating capacity building and training programs for business owners.
  • Ensuring equitable distribution of funds across all geo-political zones.
  • Implementing policies that reduce financial barriers for new and existing businesses.

Unified SME acts as a key channel for these government-backed financial interventions, ensuring transparency and accessibility for all qualified Nigerian businesses.
